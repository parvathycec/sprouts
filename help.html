PLAY GUIDE


  1. The game is played by two players

  2. Players take turns, where each turn consists of drawing a line between two spots (or from a spot to itself).

  3. The line must be a curve and must not touch or cross itself or any other line. This is important to remember!

  4. A spot cannot have more than three lines leading to or from it.

  5. The idea is to make it impossible for the other player to draw a line.

  6. So the last person to draw a line is the winner.
